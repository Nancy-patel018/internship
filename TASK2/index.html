Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About Him

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was one of India's most respected scientists and served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He played a major role in India's missile and nuclear weapons programs and was known as the "Missile Man of India".

Why I Admire Him

I admire Dr. Kalam because of his dedication, humility, and passion for education. Despite achieving great success, he remained simple and always motivated young people to dream big.
